About us


Dhruva Academy is a state of the art coaching institute located at Girinagar. Our mission is to provide a strong understanding of fundamental concepts in Science and Maths to High School students so that they can excel in the fields they choose beyond High School





Courses

Why Dhruva Academy



PPT Based Teaching
  • Use of Power point presentations for teaching
  • They make teaching more Visual and Interactive.
  • Time saved(since no writing or erasing time on board)
  • Help go back and forth through concepts easily

Low student strength

  • A low student strength of 20 per batch
  • This helps provide personalized attention to students


Online Tests
  • 10 - 15 min tests (through Dhruva Academy App)
  • Will have MCQ's, Fill in the blanks & True/False
  • Helps students revise concepts and self evaluate


Custom Notes

  • Custom notes made by the faculty at Dhruva Academy
  • A useful reference for students during Exams


Weekly Class Tests

  • Class tests to know the understanding of students
  • Create an exam environment to help students prepare for their school tests


Video Lectures

  • On demand video classes for students to revisit concepts (through Dhruva Academy App)
